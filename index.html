Privacy Policy - Cikgu Homework

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Cikgu Homework collects limited information to provide our homework assistance service:

  • Email address for account creation
  • Child's school year level (1-6) for age-appropriate explanations
  • Homework images you upload for processing
  • App usage analytics to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Generate parent teaching guides for homework questions
  • Personalize explanations based on your child's year level
  • Provide customer support and service improvements
  • Process subscription payments securely

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of all data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data retention - images are processed and deleted

4. Third-Party Services

We use these trusted services to provide our functionality:

  • Google Firebase (authentication & database)
  • Google Gemini AI (homework explanations)
  • Google ML Kit (text recognition from images)
  • Payment processors (Google Play, Touch 'n Go)

5. Children's Privacy

Cikgu Homework is designed for parents, not children:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • The app is intended for parent use to help their children
  • Parents maintain control over all account information

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export your data upon request

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:

Email: support@cikguhomework.com

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
